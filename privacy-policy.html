How StrikeZone handles your information

This page explains what registration and contact details we collect, how we use that information, and how families can request updates or removal where appropriate.

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Privacy Policy

StrikeZone Cricket League ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you register for our league or use our website.

Information We Collect

  • Player and parent or guardian names
  • Date of birth
  • Contact details, including email address and WhatsApp number
  • Player details such as age group and role
  • Referral source or how you heard about us
  • Consent and agreements covering photography, rules, and indemnity

How We Use Your Information

  • To process player registrations and manage league participation
  • To communicate fixtures, registration status, and season updates
  • To assign players to teams and age groups
  • To comply with legal, safeguarding, and operational requirements
  • To share match photos and highlights where consent has been provided

Photography and Media

With your consent, we may photograph or record players during matches and events. These images may be used on our website, social media, or promotional material. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share relevant details with league staff, coaches, and venue partners only where required for league operations, player safety, and matchday administration. We may also disclose information where required by law.

Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information. No method of online transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Your Rights

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.
  • You may withdraw consent for photography or communications at any time.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or how your data is handled, please use the details on our contact page.
