Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

StrikeZone Cricket League ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you register for our league or use our website.

Information We Collect

  • Player and parent/guardian names
  • Date of birth
  • Contact details (email address, WhatsApp number)
  • Player details (age group, role)
  • How you heard about us
  • Consent and agreements (photography, rules, indemnity)

How We Use Your Information

  • To process player registrations and manage league participation
  • To communicate important league information and updates
  • To assign players to teams and age groups
  • To comply with legal and safeguarding requirements
  • To share match photos and highlights (with consent)

Photography and Media

With your consent, we may photograph or record players during matches and events. These images may be used on our website, social media, or promotional materials. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with league staff, coaches, and venue partners only as necessary for league operations and safety. We may disclose information if required by law.

Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

  • You may request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal information.
  • You may withdraw consent for photography or communications at any time.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us via the details on our website.
